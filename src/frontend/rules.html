Game Rules

Objective

Outmaneuver your opponent, complete objectives, and control the board to win!

Basic Rules

  • Each player starts with a set of units and upgrades.
  • Players take turns moving units, attacking, and interacting with obstacles.
  • Units have unique abilities, movement, and attack ranges.
  • Obstacles and terrain affect movement and line of sight.
  • Win by completing scenario objectives or eliminating all enemy units.

Turns

  • On your turn, you may move and act with each of your units.
  • Actions include moving, attacking, searching obstacles, or using upgrades.

Victory

  • Victory conditions depend on the scenario (e.g., objectives, elimination, control).
